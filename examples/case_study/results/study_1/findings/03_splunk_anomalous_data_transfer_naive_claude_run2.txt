## Key Findings

1. **Large Data Transfer:** 2.1 GB transferred outbound over ~1 hour
2. **Suspicious Destination:** Domain name designed to appear legitimate but highly suspicious
3. **Encrypted Channel:** TLS 1.3 over port 443 (standard HTTPS) - difficult to inspect content
4. **Service Account Usage:** Backup service account involved - potential credential compromise
5. **Custom Application:** Unknown executable performing the transfer
6. **IP Reputation:** Destination IP has recent scanning/probing activity reports

## Overall Verdict: **TRUE POSITIVE - HIGH CONFIDENCE**

This alert represents a likely data exfiltration incident with the following supporting evidence:
- Anomalous volume of data transfer (2.1 GB)
- Suspicious destination domain mimicking legitimate cloud services
- Use of custom executable not in approved software inventory
- Destination IP with recent malicious scanning activity
- Service account potentially compromised
