### Recommended Next Steps
1. **Block Malicious Indicators**: Immediately block the IPs (10.0.8.55, 45.33.32.156) and the domain (suspicious-cloud-sync.com) at the firewall and proxy level.
2. **Preserve Evidence**: Retain network flow logs and proxy logs for the investigation window to support further analysis.
3. **Investigate svc_backup Account**: Conduct a detailed review of the svc_backup account's recent activities, including login times and data access patterns, to determine if it was compromised.
4. **Quantify Data Transfer**: Assess the volume of data transferred to the suspicious URL and compare it against the user's baseline to understand the scope of the exfiltration.
5. **Review DLP Alerts**: Check for any triggered Data Loss Prevention policies that might provide additional context on the data types involved.
6. **Isolate Affected Devices**: If any devices are identified as part of the data transfer, consider isolating them for forensic analysis.
7. **Notify Stakeholders**: Inform relevant stakeholders, including IT security and management, about the incident and ongoing investigation efforts.
