## Investigation Summary

### Alert Classification and Severity Assessment
- **Title**: Suspicious PowerShell Execution with Encoded Command
- **Severity**: High
- **Status**: Open
- **Tags**: PowerShell, EncodedCommand, LivingOffTheLand, T1059.001, T1027, DefenseEvasion, Execution

   - Verify the hash on VirusTotal to confirm the malware family classification.
   - Identify and interview the user (Jorge Martinez) to gather more context about the activity.

2. **Analysis**:
   - Decode any encoded PowerShell commands to identify additional C2 domains or IPs.
   - Check for any outbound connections to the identified C2 domain/IP from other hosts.

3. **Containment**:
   - Block the identified malicious domains and IPs at the firewall.
   - Ensure the endpoint agent has quarantined the malicious file.

4. **Communication**:

By following these steps, the organization can effectively contain the threat and prevent further compromise.
