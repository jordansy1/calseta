## Overall Verdict: **TRUE POSITIVE**

This alert represents a **confirmed security incident** with high confidence. The combination of:
- Anomalous data volume (2.1 GB)
- Multiple threat intelligence hits on destination IP
- Suspicious domain name
- Unknown custom application
- Service account involvement

Strongly indicates a data exfiltration attack, likely through a compromised service account or malicious insider activity.
