## Investigation Summary

### Alert Classification and Severity Assessment
- **Title**: Brute force sign-in attempts from TOR network detected
- **Severity**: High
- **Status**: Open
- **Source**: Sentinel
- **Tags**: TOR, BruteForce, IdentityThreat

The alert is classified as a high-severity identity threat due to the successful authentication following multiple failed attempts from a known TOR exit node, indicating a potential credential stuffing or brute force attack.

#### IP: 185.220.101.34

- **Risk Level**: High. The IP is a known TOR exit node with a high abuse confidence score (100) and numerous reports (2847). It is flagged as malicious by both AbuseIPDB and VirusTotal, indicating it is used for anonymized attacks.

#### DOMAIN: AAD-SIGNIN-SVC.contoso.com

### Overall Verdict
- **True Positive**: The alert is a true positive. The combination of a successful login after multiple failed attempts from a malicious TOR exit node IP and the involvement of a domain flagged for C2 activities strongly suggests a compromised account.
