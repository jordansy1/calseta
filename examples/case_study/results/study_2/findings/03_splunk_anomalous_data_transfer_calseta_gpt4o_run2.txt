### Recommended Next Steps
1. **Block Malicious Indicators**: Immediately block the IPs (10.0.8.55, 45.33.32.156) and the domain (suspicious-cloud-sync.com) on the firewall and proxy to prevent further data exfiltration.
2. **Preserve Evidence**: Retain network flow logs and proxy logs for the investigation window to support further analysis.
3. **Investigate User Account**: Conduct a detailed investigation into the svc_backup account to determine if the data transfer was initiated by a legitimate process or if the account has been compromised.
4. **Review DLP Alerts**: Check for any Data Loss Prevention alerts that may have been triggered by this data transfer.
5. **Inspect Data Transfer**: Quantify the amount of data transferred and compare it against the user's 30-day baseline to assess the anomaly.
6. **Timeline Analysis**: Analyze the timeline of the data transfer to determine if it occurred during business hours and if there were any unusual access patterns.
7. **Communicate with Stakeholders**: Inform relevant stakeholders, including the user's manager and IT security team, about the incident and ongoing investigation.
