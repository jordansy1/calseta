**Classification:** True Positive - Credential Attack via TOR
**Severity:** High (Confirmed)

This alert represents a confirmed brute force attack that successfully compromised a privileged user account. The attack pattern shows 47 failed authentication attempts followed by one successful login, all originating from a known TOR exit node, which is a classic indicator of credential stuffing or brute force attacks using anonymization networks.
